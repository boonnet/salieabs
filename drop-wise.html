ABOUT DROP WISE

Smart Water Metering Solution

Drop Wise is a comprehensive smart water metering and level monitoring solution designed to track water consumption efficiently. We offer both advanced smart meter hardware and robust metering software as a turnkey solution. Our platform is meter-agnostic, seamlessly integrating with third-party meters. Additionally, we provide reliable connectivity solutions through our suite of dataloggers, ensuring smooth communication between meters and the Drop Wise software platform.

Challenges Solved

Drop Wise is a unified solution for monitoring water consumption at tail-end points, water distribution points, and borewell exploitation, all on a single, user-friendly platform. It simplifies water management by providing comprehensive insights and real-time data, enabling efficient monitoring and control across diverse water use cases with ease.
